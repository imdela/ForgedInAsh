# Forged in Ash — _Kaerath Universe_

> _A dark romantasy series. Written under the pen name **Vera Draye**._

---

## Series Overview

**Forged in Ash** is a dark romantasy series set in the world of Kaerath — a continent governed by the politics of forge marks, empire loyalties, and an information economy built on silence.

The series follows **Ryn**, a forge-marked carrier living in the southern district of Vael, and **Calder**, an operative sent to extract her. What begins as a negotiated transaction across ten days becomes something neither character has a framework for.

**Themes:** Hidden power · Surveillance states · The cost of trust · Slow burn built on mutual recognition

**Comparable titles:** _Fourth Wing_ (Yarros) · _The Bridge Kingdom_ (Jensen) · _Six of Crows_ (Bardugo) · _From Blood and Ash_ (Armentrout)

## Production Notes

- **Language:** English (manuscript) · French (production documentation)
- **Target word count:** ~100 000 words / tome
- **Format:** 3 300 words/chapter average · 30 chapters · 4 acts
- **Spice calibration:** Warm (T1) → Moderate (T2) → Full (T3+)
- **Series differentiator:** Dual analytical POV — both protagonists observe each other with the same operational methodology. The desire lives in the data they collect on each other.
